General Hospital Spoilers for Thursday, July 23, 2026: Willow Tries to Stop Nina, Michael Changes Deception's Future, and Sonny Confronts Rocco

By Today On General Hospital • Published July 23, 2026 • General Hospital Spoilers

Thursday's episode of General Hospital is packed with emotional confrontations, shocking decisions, and major business developments. Willow and Nina struggle to agree on Drew's future, Michael's latest move could transform Deception, Sonny reaches out to Rocco, and Anna continues searching for the truth behind the missing evidence.

Willow and Nina Disagree Over Drew's Future

Willow refuses to let Nina take extreme action against Drew. Although Nina believes they need to act before Drew causes more damage, Willow insists there are better ways to handle the situation without making a dangerous mistake.

She urges Nina to stay calm and reminds her that they can control the situation without crossing the line. However, Willow's words may not be enough to stop Nina from following her own instincts.

Nina's Dangerous Move Could Change Everything

Nina's plan quickly becomes more dangerous than anyone expected. She arranges to meet Drew near the lake while Scout is practicing her swimming, hoping to deal with him privately.

During the encounter, Drew unexpectedly speaks, leaving Nina completely stunned. The tense confrontation appears ready to end in disaster, with Drew seemingly headed toward the water. If Nina loses control in the heat of the moment, this could become one of the biggest shocks of the week.

Michael's New Deal Could Shake Up Deception

While family drama unfolds elsewhere, Michael focuses on business. He meets with Alexis to discuss important legal matters that could be connected to his growing role at Deception.

After Maxie approaches him with a surprising proposal, Michael appears ready to take control of a significant share of the company. His involvement could completely change Deception's leadership and create fresh tension among everyone connected to the business. During the same storyline, Maxie's agreement with Michael could leave Tracy, Lucy, and Brook Lynn scrambling as they react to the unexpected changes inside Deception.

Tracy Accuses Lucy of Manipulation

Tracy refuses to trust Lucy and believes her longtime rival may be using James' grief to influence Maxie. She directly questions Lucy's intentions and wonders if she's taking advantage of an emotional child to gain control over Deception.

The confrontation between the two women becomes increasingly personal, showing that their rivalry is far from over. With Michael's deal already creating uncertainty, Tracy believes Lucy may be willing to do whatever it takes to protect her own interests.

Sonny Opens Up to Rocco About a Difficult Secret

Away from the Deception drama, Sonny asks Rocco to spend a few minutes alone with him for an honest conversation.

Sonny gently reveals that he already knows Rocco was involved in the shooting of Ross Cullum. Instead of criticizing him, Sonny reminds his grandson that he will always have someone to turn to whenever he's ready to talk. The emotional conversation could become an important step in helping Rocco deal with everything that has happened.

Laura Warns Dante About the Missing Evidence

Laura grows increasingly concerned about the missing evidence connected to the Ross Cullum case. She meets with Dante and warns him that the unexplained disappearance of important records could eventually create serious problems for everyone involved.

With Anna already questioning what happened to the evidence, Laura believes the truth may not stay hidden much longer. If the investigation continues, the entire cover-up could begin to unravel.

Anna Tests Chase's Loyalty

Anna continues her investigation by turning to Harrison Chase. Before sharing anything important, she makes it clear that she needs to know she can trust him completely.

Chase now has an opportunity to prove himself by helping Anna uncover what really happened to the missing evidence. If he succeeds, this assignment could become the first step toward rebuilding his reputation within the PCPD.

Chase's Career Could Finally Get Back on Track

As Anna's investigation moves forward, Chase may finally receive another chance to revive his law enforcement career. Successfully solving the missing evidence mystery could help him earn another opportunity to pursue detective work and regain the position he has been working toward.

With multiple secrets threatening to come to light, Chase's next move could have a major impact on Port Charles.

Final Thoughts

Thursday's General Hospital episode delivers everything fans love: family conflict, emotional conversations, business power struggles, and an investigation that could expose long-hidden secrets. Willow fights to keep Nina from making a terrible mistake, Michael's Deception deal changes the company's future, Sonny offers support to Rocco during a difficult time, and Anna moves one step closer to uncovering the truth.
