About Us

Welcome to Today On General Hospital, your daily destination for everything happening in Port Charles. We are an independent fan site created by passionate viewers who love the long-running ABC soap opera General Hospital and want to share that excitement with fellow fans around the world.

Our mission is simple: to give General Hospital fans a fast, friendly, and reliable place to catch up on the show. Every day we publish the latest spoilers so you know what is coming next, detailed episode recaps so you never miss a moment, and the freshest news covering casting updates, cast interviews, and behind-the-scenes buzz. Whether you watch every single episode or only tune in when you can, our goal is to keep you fully in the loop.

We organize everything into three easy categories, Spoilers, Recaps, and News, so you can find exactly what you are looking for in seconds. Our content is written in a clear, conversational style, packed with photos, and designed to look great on both your phone and your computer.

Today On General Hospital started as a Facebook community of dedicated fans, and it has grown into a home where thousands of viewers gather every day to discuss their favorite storylines, couples, and characters. This website is a natural extension of that community, carefully built to serve you even better with faster updates and easier browsing on any device.

Please note that we are an unofficial fan site. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to ABC, Disney, or the official General Hospital production in any way. All trademarks and copyrights belong to their respective owners. We simply celebrate the show we love. Thank you for being part of our community, and enjoy your stay.
