Contact Us

We love hearing from fellow General Hospital fans. Whether you have a question, some feedback, a story tip, or a business enquiry, the Today On General Hospital team is happy to help.

The best way to reach us is by email. We read every message and do our best to reply as quickly as possible, usually within a couple of business days. If you have spotted a factual error in one of our posts or want to suggest a topic you would like us to cover, please let us know, your input helps us make the site better for everyone.

Please keep in mind that we are a fan-run site and not the official General Hospital production, ABC, or Disney. We are unable to pass messages to the cast, arrange autographs, or answer questions about the show's official schedule. For anything related to the broadcast itself, please reach out to the network directly. For everything else, from correction requests to partnership ideas, we are all ears.

For the fastest updates and to join the conversation with thousands of other fans, follow our Facebook page. We post daily spoilers, recaps and news there too, and it is the quickest way to reach us and stay connected with the community.
