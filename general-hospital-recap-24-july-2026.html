General Hospital Recap for Friday, July 24: Jason and Britt Reunite While Nina Pushes Drew Into the Lake

By Today On General Hospital • Published July 24, 2026 • General Hospital Recap

Port Charles delivered another unforgettable episode on Friday, July 24, packed with emotional family moments, shocking betrayals, and one jaw-dropping ending. Jason and Britt finally crossed paths again, Willow prepared to disappear with her children, Valentin tried to strike a dangerous bargain, and Nina reached her breaking point with Drew. Here's a complete recap of everything that happened.

Jason Returns Home and Notices Willow's Secret Plans

Jason Morgan and Danny returned home after their road trip and immediately walked into growing tension at the boathouse. Willow was there with Nina, Drew, Scout, and Rocco, but it didn't take Jason long to sense something wasn't right.

Jason hoped to speak with Willow about Danny and Scout's future living arrangements, but she brushed him off, saying she had more important matters to deal with. His suspicions only grew stronger when Willow privately asked Nina to take Drew home while she contacted the WSB.

Jason Encourages Peace Between the Families

Scout admitted she wished everyone in the family could stop fighting. Her heartfelt words convinced Jason that continuing the feud with Drew wasn't helping anyone, especially their children.

Putting the kids first, Jason admitted that both brothers needed to move forward instead of holding onto old resentment.

Jason Helps Rocco Leave the Past Behind

Jason Takes Responsibility for the Shooting

Concerned about Rocco's guilt over the shooting involving Cullum, Jason reassured the teenager that he would accept any consequences if the WSB ever investigated the incident.

Jason reminded Rocco that his actions had actually saved both Jason and Britt that night. Rather than living with guilt, Jason encouraged him to focus on building a better future.

Scout Defends Her Father as Danny Makes Peace

Back at the dock, Scout happily explained how much she loved spending time with Danny and hoped their families could continue living peacefully together.

However, Danny made a sarcastic remark about Drew, upsetting Scout. Nina immediately stepped in, reminding everyone that Drew loved his daughter and wanted what was best for her.

Realising he'd gone too far, Danny apologised both for his comment and for how he'd treated Drew in the past. He also promised Scout she could always count on him.

Elizabeth Pushes Willow to Make an Important Decision

Elizabeth visited Willow at the office to ask why Drew still hadn't received his communication device.

Willow admitted she'd delayed the process because she wasn't emotionally ready to face Drew while he communicated through the device. After Liz insisted it couldn't wait any longer, Willow finally agreed to have it installed the following week.

Jack Delivers Devastating News

Just after Elizabeth left, Willow received an unexpected phone call from Jack Brennan. He revealed that the missing black box still hadn't been found, meaning there was no longer any evidence that could be used against Drew.

Feeling responsible for Nina's situation, Jack offered Willow a dramatic escape plan. He promised to arrange a flight that very night for Willow, Wiley, and Amelia, complete with brand-new identities. Shaken by the news, Willow immediately phoned Nina and asked her to leave Port Charles with them.

Dante and Chase Begin Repairing Their Partnership

Willow Is Stopped Before She Can Escape

Knowing Anna wanted Chase back as his detective partner, Dante decided it was time to rebuild their working relationship.

They discussed Ezra's claims linking Willow to the Sidwell investigation. After weighing the situation, Chase agreed it was necessary to bring Willow in for questioning. Unfortunately for Willow, Chase arrived at her office just as she was preparing to flee town with her children.

Valentin Makes a Dangerous Proposal

At Bobbie's, Valentin apologised to Alexis for the trouble his time on the run had caused her.

Although Alexis encouraged him to start over, Valentin admitted he had unfinished business. His priority was recovering everything he had lost, and he intended to use the WSB to make it happen. Later, Valentin confronted Jack Brennan with an offer. He claimed he could return the missing black box, but only if the WSB gave him something valuable in return.

Britt Faces Trouble at General Hospital

Britt's late arrival at the hospital didn't go unnoticed.

Lucas criticised her for always acting like the victim and warned that everyone was growing tired of her excuses. He even admitted he preferred the version of Britt who had shown more humility while facing her illness.

The harsh conversation forced Britt to reflect on her behaviour. She later apologised to Elizabeth for failing to notify anyone that she would be late, although she became frustrated after learning Liz had already reported the incident to HR.

Britt and Jason Finally Reunite

As Britt walked through the hospital, she briefly noticed Elizabeth and Lucas searching apartment listings together. Moments later, everything else faded away.

Britt looked up and saw Jason walking through the hospital entrance. The emotional reunion left her smiling, marking one of the episode's biggest moments.

Nina Makes the Most Shocking Decision Yet

Drew Ends Up in the Lake

After Scout and Danny left to meet Alexis, Nina found herself completely alone with Drew at the boathouse. Before making any move, she desperately called Jack Brennan to confirm whether Willow had been telling the truth, that they no longer had any leverage against Drew.

Then everything changed. As Nina unlocked the wheels of Drew's wheelchair, he suddenly spoke, mocking her and asking whether she'd just received bad news. Keeping her emotions under control, Nina replied that the news was even worse for him.

Without another warning, she shoved Drew and his wheelchair straight off the dock and into the lake. The shocking cliffhanger left Drew fighting for his life and Nina facing consequences that could change everything.

Final Thoughts

Friday's episode delivered everything General Hospital fans could ask for: family drama, emotional reunions, dangerous secrets, and an explosive ending. Jason and Britt's unexpected reunion offered hope, while Nina's desperate decision to eliminate Drew may become one of the biggest turning points of the summer. With Willow's escape plan falling apart and the black box mystery still unresolved, the fallout is only just beginning.
