Is Drew Leaving General Hospital in 2026? Here's What Nina's Shocking Attack Really Means

By Today On General Hospital • Published July 25, 2026 • General Hospital News

Fans were left stunned after General Hospital's July 24 cliffhanger, when Nina Reeves pushed Drew Cain and his wheelchair straight into the water. The dramatic moment immediately sparked one question across social media: Is Drew leaving General Hospital in 2026?

The short answer is no, there is currently no official confirmation that Cameron Mathison is leaving the soap. Recent reports have confirmed that the actor remains part of the cast, despite multiple death scares for Drew.

Nina Finally Crosses the Line

Drew has survived more than most residents of Port Charles. After months trapped in a locked-in state, being shot, and enduring paralysis, he had finally begun showing signs of recovery. Unknown to nearly everyone, Drew could now move and even speak a few words.

That secret became dangerous when Nina approached him near the Quartermaine docks. After receiving troubling news about Brennan and the missing black box, Nina released Drew's wheelchair brake. Just as he revealed he could speak by asking if she'd received bad news, Nina coldly answered: "Yes, for me. Worse for you." Then she shoved him into the water.

It was one of the most shocking cliffhangers General Hospital has delivered this year.

Could Drew Actually Die?

At first glance, Drew's chances don't look good. Months of paralysis have left him physically weak, and if he cannot swim or pull himself to safety, drowning becomes a real possibility.

However, there are several reasons fans shouldn't assume Drew is dead just yet. His recovery has been progressing faster than anyone realizes. Recent episodes have shown him regaining movement in his hands, speaking again, and secretly communicating with others. If his strength has returned more than Nina believes, he may still be capable of saving himself, or at least calling for help before it's too late.

Is Cameron Mathison Leaving General Hospital?

Despite the intense speculation, there is no evidence that Cameron Mathison is exiting General Hospital. Earlier this year, rumours spread online claiming Drew's storyline was leading to the actor's departure. Those reports were later debunked, with reliable entertainment outlets confirming Mathison was not leaving the series.

As of now:

  • No official exit announcement has been made.
  • ABC has not confirmed Drew's departure.
  • Cameron Mathison remains listed among the show's active cast.
  • The drowning storyline appears to be another major cliffhanger rather than a confirmed farewell.

What Happens Next?

The bigger mystery isn't whether Drew falls into the water, it's whether someone reaches him in time. With Brennan searching for the missing evidence, Willow desperate to protect her secrets, and Nina realizing the consequences of what she's done, the fallout could change several lives in Port Charles.

If Drew survives, Nina could face attempted murder charges and expose an even larger conspiracy surrounding the black box. If he doesn't, the entire balance of power in Port Charles could shift overnight.

Final Verdict

At this point, Drew is in grave danger, but fans should not assume this is his final episode. The cliffhanger is designed to leave viewers guessing, and there is currently no official confirmation that Cameron Mathison is leaving General Hospital in 2026. Until ABC or the actor announces an exit, Drew's fate remains one of the show's biggest unanswered questions.
